Our Approach

We get to know you and your needs. We collaborate with you in mapping a research plan per your desired outcomes, your timeline, your budget. We like to keep you, the client, involved in the process. Working with you, we identify gaps in your customer/market knowedge. It is important to validate what you aleady know but we want our reserach to provide you with new and relevant insight. We want you be be able to put yourself in your customers' shoes. We work to enable you to: Identify your real target customer; Know the decision-maker; Get familiar with your customers' world; Understand their environment; See your customers' day-to-day reality; Map your customers' pain points and address their needs.

What we do

  • Immerse ourselves in your customers' environment. Observe daily practices, habits and challenges.
  • Map customer pain points. Identify customer needs.
  • Analzye, empathize and prioritize. Validate research findings.
  • Provide you with deep insight into your customer.

Ethnography

Deep immersion the customer environment: A prolonged time is spent in customer environment, observing, documenting, engaging, realizing and understanding their world.

Contextual Interviewing

One-to-one interviews with your customers: Open-ended interviewing that allows the customer to express themselves and allows you to gain a deeper insight into their day-to-day, their perceptions, opinions and unmet needs.

Customer Profiling

Each customer is profiled: For each customer we chart the tasks they are doing, what outcome they desire and how they currently achieve this. We map their perception, attitude and behaviour.

Simulations

Simulating the customer environment with changes to test concepts: Concepts and prototypes are tested real-time with the intended user in the intended environment.

Data Analysis and Validation

Multi-method data analysis: Various techniques are used to translate the data gathered into deep customer insight. Preferably data analysis is conducted with you, the client, an expert in your field. Workshops with your team are recommended. We also like to validate the outcomes with the customer via additional qualitative/quantitive research: Focus Groups; Prototype Simulations; Surveys, etc.

Story-telling and Stakeholder Management

Effective story-telling is an essential part of the project. We know because we have been there. You need to present the research outcomes in a meaningful and compelling way to your stakeholders. Wheither you are a start-up looking for funding or vying for budget allocation in a large organization we can help you sell the story.
